About VetCare Pro

Caring for Johannesburg's pets since 2014

We're a small, dedicated team who believe great veterinary care starts with genuinely listening to both pets and their owners.

Our Story

VetCare Pro started in 2014 as a single-vet practice in Sandton, built on a simple idea: pet owners deserve clear answers, not jargon, and pets deserve care that doesn't feel rushed. Over the past decade we've grown into a full clinic with a small team of vets and support staff, but that original approach hasn't changed.

We see everything from routine check-ups to more involved procedures, and we built this website so booking and managing those visits is as straightforward as the care itself.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, honest, and compassionate veterinary care — and to treat every animal that comes through our doors the way we'd want our own pets treated.

Our Values

  • Clear communication, no unnecessary jargon
  • Treating every pet and owner with patience and respect
  • Keeping appointments and records simple to manage
  • Honest pricing, explained before any procedure
