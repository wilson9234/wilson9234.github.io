Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book in advance?

Yes, we recommend booking online ahead of time to guarantee a slot with your preferred vet. Walk-ins are accommodated where possible but may involve a wait.

What should I bring to a vaccination appointment?

Please bring your pet's existing vaccination card if you have one, so we can keep your pet's records accurate and on schedule.

Are prices negotiable or fixed?

Listed prices are starting estimates for standard cases. Your vet will always confirm the final price with you before carrying out any procedure that costs more than the estimate.

Can I pay online after my appointment?

Yes — as soon as you book, an invoice is created in your dashboard, which you can pay online from there at any time.
